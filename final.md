# Predicate

* Negation of quantified statements:
  * ~(∀x ∈ D, p(x)) ≡ ∃x ∈ D, ~p(x)
  * ~(∃x ∈ D, p(x)) ≡ ∀x ∈ D, ~p(x)

* Negation of universal conditional statements
  * ~((∀x ∈ D, p(x)) ≡ ∃x, ~(p(x) → q(x)) ≡ ∃x, (p(x) ∧ ~q(x))
